Information We Collect

  • Health & Nutrition Data: Meals logged, nutrition information (calories, macronutrients, vitamins, minerals), dietary preferences, and health metrics you choose to share. This includes data entered manually, via voice, or through photo recognition.

  • Camera Access: Photos are processed locally for meal recognition and are never stored on our servers

Apple HealthKit Integration (iOS)

If you connect Apple HealthKit:

  • We read: Active calories burned
  • We write: Dietary calories and macros from logged meals
  • This data stays on your device unless you enable cloud backup
  • HealthKit data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for purposes unrelated to the app's health tracking features
ü§ñ

Google Health Connect Integration (Android)

If you connect Google Health Connect, HealthyOne accesses the following data types:

  • Active Calories Burned: Read to display your daily calorie burn and populate the Move activity ring
  • Exercise Sessions: Read to display your workout summaries and calculate exercise minutes for the Exercise activity ring
  • Steps: Read to display your daily step count and approximate your Stand activity ring
  • Total Calories Burned: Read to provide comprehensive calorie tracking alongside your dietary intake

How we use Health Connect data:

  • All Health Connect data is used exclusively to power in-app features such as activity rings, workout summaries, and calorie balance calculations
  • Health Connect data is displayed only within the app and is not shared with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers
  • Health Connect data is not used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to health and fitness tracking
  • You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time through your device's Health Connect settings

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.
  • Meal & Nutrition Logs: Stored locally on your device indefinitely until you delete them. Cloud-synced data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Data accessed from HealthKit or Health Connect is used in real-time within the app and is not independently stored on our servers. Any cached fitness data on your device is cleared when you log out or delete the app.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized usage analytics may be retained for up to 24 months to improve app performance and features.
  • After Account Deletion: All personally identifiable data, including meal logs, nutrition history, and account information, is permanently deleted within 30 days of your deletion request. Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytical purposes.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. Here's how:

  • In-App Deletion: Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data, including meal logs, nutrition history, and profile information.
  • Email Request: Send a deletion request to support@healthyoneapp.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your account.
  • Local Data: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device, including cached Health Connect and HealthKit data.
  • Health Connect Data: You can revoke HealthyOne's access to Health Connect data at any time through your Android device's Settings ‚Üí Health Connect ‚Üí HealthyOne ‚Üí Disconnect. This does not delete data already synced to the Health Connect platform by other apps.

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will process it within 30 days and confirm deletion via email. Please note that some data may be retained briefly in encrypted backups before being permanently purged.

SMS & Text Messaging

HealthyOne may send SMS text messages for the following purposes:

  • Account Verification: One-time codes to verify your phone number during account setup
  • Meal Reminders: Optional reminders to log your meals (only if you opt in)
  • Important Account Alerts: Security notifications and critical account updates

Your consent: By providing your phone number and enabling SMS features in the app, you consent to receive text messages from HealthyOne. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your settings.

Opt-out: You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message, disabling SMS notifications in the app settings, or contacting us at support@healthyoneapp.com. We will process your opt-out request promptly.

No sharing: Your phone number is never sold, shared with third parties for marketing purposes, or used for any purpose other than the services described above.
