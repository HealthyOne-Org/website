Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect it.

Last Updated: February 2026

HealthyOne ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the HealthyOne app and related services.

📱

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information (optional)

Health & Nutrition Data

  • Meal logs and food items you record
  • Calories, macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat), and micronutrients (vitamins, minerals)
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Health metrics you manually enter
  • Data from voice logging and photo analysis

Fitness & Activity Data

  • Active calories burned (from HealthKit/Health Connect)
  • Exercise sessions and duration
  • Steps and total daily calories burned
  • Only collected with your explicit permission

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and crash logs
  • Unique device identifier

Camera Access

  • Photos are processed locally on your device to extract nutrition information
  • Photos are never stored on our servers
  • Photo analysis is performed using on-device AI processing
🤖

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide & Improve Service: To deliver HealthyOne features, sync your data across devices, and improve app performance
  • Nutrition Recommendations: To calculate personalized nutrition recommendations and health insights based on your logged meals and activity
  • Service Updates: To send you important account and service notifications (separately from optional SMS reminders)
  • AI Processing: Your meals and photos are processed by AI to extract nutritional information. This data is used only to improve your in-app experience and is never used to train global AI models or shared with external AI services
  • Analytics: To understand how the app is used and identify areas for improvement (anonymized and aggregated)
🔒

Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to our servers uses industry-standard TLS encryption
  • Local Storage: Your health and nutrition data is stored locally on your device by default
  • Cloud Backup (Optional): You may optionally enable cloud backup via Supabase to sync data across devices. You control this setting
  • Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits and follow OWASP best practices
  • Sensitive Data Protection: Health and fitness data is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud backup
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text
🌐

Third-Party Services

HealthyOne uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: PostgreSQL database for cloud backup (optional)
  • Edamam API: Nutrition database for food lookup
  • USDA Food Database: Nutritional information
  • Google ML Kit: On-device AI processing for photo analysis
  • Twilio: SMS delivery for optional text message reminders

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We only share data necessary to provide the services you've requested.

🚫

Data Sharing

  • No Data Sales: We never sell your personal or health data to third parties for marketing or any other purpose
  • Consent-Based Sharing: We only share data with third parties with your explicit consent or as required by law
  • Service Providers: We may share data with service providers (like Supabase) only to the extent necessary to provide HealthyOne services
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government request
📱

SMS & Text Messaging

HealthyOne offers optional SMS text message notifications to help you stay on track with your nutrition goals. Here's how our SMS service works:

What Messages You'll Receive

  • Optional meal logging reminders to help you remember to track your nutrition
  • Return-to-habit nudges if you haven't logged in for a while
  • Account-related notifications (verification codes, security alerts)
  • Messages are never promotional or spammy

How You Opt In

  • SMS reminders are OFF by default — you must explicitly enable them
  • In the HealthyOne app, go to Settings → Notifications → SMS Reminders
  • When you tap to enable, a disclosure screen appears explaining message types and frequency
  • You must tap "Enable SMS Reminders" to confirm your consent
  • You may also opt in by texting START to our messaging number

Message Frequency

  • Messages are low frequency (typically 1-3 per week maximum)
  • Messages are transactional and not promotional
  • Message frequency may vary based on your app usage patterns and settings
  • You control the frequency in app settings

How to Opt Out

  • Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe from all SMS
  • Or disable SMS Reminders in the app: Settings → Notifications → SMS Reminders → OFF
  • You can also email support@healthyoneapp.com to request removal from SMS messaging
  • Opting out is immediate and does not affect other app notifications

Costs

  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan
  • HealthyOne does not charge any additional fees for SMS messages
  • Contact your carrier for details about your message and data rates

Privacy & Phone Number Protection

  • Your phone number is used solely for sending the notifications you opted into
  • We never share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Phone numbers are stored securely and deleted when you opt out or delete your account
  • SMS delivery is handled by Twilio, a trusted third-party SMS provider
  • Your phone number is transmitted securely to Twilio only for message delivery

Questions about SMS? Contact support@healthyoneapp.com with any questions about our SMS service, including how to manage your preferences or opt out.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Request Deletion: Ask us to delete your account and associated data
  • Export Your History: Export your meal logs and health data
  • Opt Out of Cloud Backup: Disable cloud sync at any time (data remains on your device)
  • Revoke Health Permissions: Disconnect HealthKit (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) at any time through your device settings
  • Opt Out of Analytics: Disable analytics tracking in app settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@healthyoneapp.com.

❤️

Apple HealthKit Integration (iOS)

  • Permissions: HealthyOne reads active calories burned and writes dietary calories/macros to your HealthKit
  • Local Storage: HealthKit data stays on your device unless you enable cloud backup
  • Never Sold: HealthKit data is never sold, shared with marketers, or used for any purpose other than improving your HealthyOne experience
  • User Control: You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices
  • Apple Compliance: We comply fully with Apple's HealthKit requirements and never access restricted health categories
🤖

Google Health Connect Integration (Android)

  • Data Read: Active calories burned, exercise sessions, steps, and total daily calories
  • Purpose: Used exclusively for in-app features to provide personalized nutrition and activity insights
  • Never Shared: Health Connect data is not shared with third parties for any reason
  • User Control: You can disconnect Health Connect at any time in Android Settings → Health → Manage apps & device access
  • Google Compliance: We comply with Google's Health Connect policies and data handling requirements
🕐

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted 30 days after you request account deletion
  • Meal Logs: Stored locally on your device indefinitely. If cloud backup is enabled, stored in Supabase and synced
  • Health Data: Real-time sync only. Not stored on our servers unless cloud backup is explicitly enabled
  • Analytics: Aggregated analytics data retained for 24 months to track app improvements
  • Post-Deletion: We retain minimal data for 30 days to finalize account closure, then permanently delete all records
  • Local Device Data: Deleted when you uninstall the app (unless you backed up data to iCloud/Google Drive)
🗑️

Data Deletion

Delete Your Account In-App

  • Open the HealthyOne app
  • Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Enter your password and confirm deletion
  • Your account will be marked for deletion immediately

Email Deletion Request

  • Email support@healthyoneapp.com with your account email and request to delete
  • We'll verify your identity and process the deletion

What Happens After Deletion

  • Local app data is removed from your device
  • Cloud backup data (if enabled) is permanently deleted from Supabase
  • Your HealthKit/Health Connect permissions remain under your device's control
  • Processing takes up to 30 days due to backup systems

Disconnect HealthKit/Health Connect

  • iOS: Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Find HealthyOne → Toggle off permissions
  • Android: Settings → Health → Manage apps & device access → Find HealthyOne → Disconnect

Children's Privacy

HealthyOne is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account immediately.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact support@healthyoneapp.com immediately.
