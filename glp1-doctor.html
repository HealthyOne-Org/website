Frequently asked questions

Is GLP-1 right for me?

That's a medical decision and only a licensed doctor can answer it. Each provider above will evaluate your health history, current medications, BMI, and goals before deciding. GLP-1 medications aren't appropriate for everyone — for example, people with certain thyroid conditions or a personal/family history of medullary thyroid cancer should not take them.

Do I need insurance?

No. All four partners offer cash-pay options. Some may run an insurance check on your behalf if you'd like, but coverage for GLP-1 medications for weight loss varies widely and is often denied. Cash-pay pricing is shown on each provider's site after the initial visit.

Why call it a 2-for-1?

Because losing weight and staying healthy are two different problems. A GLP-1 program solves the first — it shrinks appetite and drops pounds. HealthyOne solves the second — it makes sure you're getting enough protein to protect muscle, keeps an eye on sodium and heart-health markers, and gives you a single dashboard to see where you actually are. One purchase covers prescription + clinical care. The other is the nutrition tracker free on the App Store and Google Play. Together they cost less than most weight-loss coaching programs and give you more.
