About HealthyOne

AI-powered nutrition tracking that makes healthy eating effortless — built by Hardaway Labs

🎯

Our Mission

HealthyOne exists to make nutrition tracking so simple that anyone can do it — and actually stick with it. We believe that understanding what you eat shouldn't require a nutrition degree, hours of manual logging, or expensive coaching.

Our AI-powered app lets you track meals in seconds using just your voice or a photo. No more searching food databases or guessing portion sizes. Just speak or snap, and HealthyOne does the rest.

📖

Our Story

HealthyOne was created by Christopher Hardaway, founder of Hardaway Labs. After years of trying every nutrition app on the market and finding them all tedious to use, Chris set out to build something better — an app that removes the friction from food tracking using the latest in AI technology.

The result is HealthyOne: a nutrition tracker that listens when you talk, sees when you snap a photo, and learns your habits to help you build a healthier relationship with food. No judgment, no guilt — just smart, effortless tracking.

✨

What Makes Us Different

  • Voice Logging: Say what you ate and our AI instantly logs the nutrition — calories, macros, sodium, and more
  • Photo Analysis: Snap a photo of your meal and get a full nutritional breakdown in seconds
  • Heart Health Tracking: Monitor sodium intake and heart health metrics alongside your nutrition
  • No Manual Entry: We eliminate the tedious part of food tracking so you can focus on eating well
  • Privacy First: Your data is encrypted, never sold, and you can delete it anytime
