SMS Text Message Opt-In

HealthyOne offers optional SMS nutrition reminders to help you stay on track with your health goals. SMS messaging is entirely voluntary and is not required to use the HealthyOne app.

Important: SMS reminders are completely optional. You can sign up for HealthyOne and use all app features without ever providing a phone number or enabling text messages. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of purchase or use of the app.

How You Opt In to SMS Reminders

To receive SMS nutrition reminders from HealthyOne, you must take the following steps inside the app:

  • Step 1: Open the HealthyOne app and go to Settings → SMS Notifications.
  • Step 2: Enter your mobile phone number in the phone number field.
  • Step 3: Check the consent checkbox to agree to receive text messages from HealthyOne. This checkbox is unchecked by default.
  • Step 4: Tap "Enable SMS Reminders" to submit.
  • Step 5: Verify your phone number by entering the one-time verification code sent to your phone via text.

No messages are sent until you complete all steps above, including phone number verification.

What Messages Will You Receive?

If you opt in, you will receive transactional nutrition reminder messages only. These include:

  • Meal logging reminders (e.g., "You haven't logged a meal today")
  • Return-to-habit nudges after periods of inactivity
  • Account verification codes

We do not send promotional, marketing, or advertising messages via SMS.

Sample Message

"HealthyOne Reminder: You haven't logged a meal today. Open the app to track your nutrition! Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help."

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time using any of these methods:

  • Reply STOP to any text message from HealthyOne
  • In the app: Go to Settings → SMS Notifications → Remove Phone Number
  • Email support@healthyoneapp.com

After opting out, you will receive a confirmation: "You've been unsubscribed and will no longer receive SMS reminders from HealthyOne. Reply START to re-subscribe."

HELP Response

Reply HELP to any message to receive:

"HealthyOne SMS Help: For support, visit healthyoneapp.com or email support@healthyoneapp.com. Reply STOP to opt out of messages. Msg & data rates may apply."

Message Frequency & Costs

  • Frequency: Approximately 1–3 messages per week. Frequency varies based on your usage and reminder settings.
  • Costs: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier. HealthyOne does not charge any fees for SMS messages.

Your Privacy

  • Your phone number is used solely for delivering the SMS notifications you opted into.
  • We never share your phone number with third parties for marketing or any other purpose.
  • Phone numbers are stored securely and deleted when you opt out or delete your account.
  • For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
