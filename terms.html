Description of Service

HealthyOne is a meal logging and nutrition tracking application available on iOS and Android. The App provides tools and features to help you monitor your nutrition and health, including:

  • Voice & Text Logging: Log meals by speaking, typing, or searching our food database
  • Photo Analysis: Photograph your meals to automatically extract nutritional information
  • Calorie & Macro Tracking: Track calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, and other macronutrients
  • Heart Health Tracking: Monitor cardiovascular metrics and health indicators
  • Recipe Discovery & Saving: Search, discover, and save recipes for future reference
  • Progress Monitoring: View charts and analytics of your nutrition and health progress

HealthyOne is intended as a personal health and wellness tool. The features and services provided are "as is" and may be updated or modified at our discretion.
