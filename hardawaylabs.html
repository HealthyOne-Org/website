Meet the Founder

CH

Christopher Hardaway

Founder & CEO, Hardaway Labs LLC

After years of trying every health and nutrition app on the market and finding them all tedious, Christopher set out to build something better. Armed with a passion for technology and a belief that AI can make healthy living accessible to everyone, he founded Hardaway Labs to create the tools he wished existed.

Today, Hardaway Labs is building a growing ecosystem of "Healthy" apps — each designed with the same core philosophy: use cutting-edge AI to remove friction, respect privacy, and empower people to live their healthiest lives without judgment or guilt.

Our Mission

Hardaway Labs exists to make wellness technology that actually works for real people. We believe that tracking your health — whether it's nutrition, emotional fitness, or finances — shouldn't feel like a chore. Our apps leverage the latest in AI to deliver effortless, accurate, and private experiences that help you build better habits and live well.

What We Stand For

Privacy by Default

Your data is yours. We never sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything other than delivering your experience. Local-first by design.

Effortless by Design

Every interaction should take seconds, not minutes. We obsess over removing friction so healthy habits feel natural.

AI-Powered Accuracy

We use cutting-edge AI — including Google Gemini — to deliver precise, intelligent experiences. No more guessing or manual data entry.

Grace Over Guilt

We don't shame you for slip-ups. Our apps meet you where you are with encouragement, not judgment. Progress, not perfection.
